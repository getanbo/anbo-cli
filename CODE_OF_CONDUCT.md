# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

We want participation in Anbo Stack to be safe, respectful, and productive.
Examples of welcome behavior include constructive technical feedback,
acknowledging tradeoffs, respecting differing experience levels, and taking
responsibility for mistakes.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, publishing private information, and sustained disruption are not
acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. They
will aim to consider context, impact, and a proportionate response while
protecting the privacy and safety of people who report concerns.

Report enforcement concerns through the repository's private
[security-advisory form](https://github.com/getanbo/anbo-stack/security/advisories/new)
and prefix the title with `Conduct:`. Do not use a public issue for a sensitive
report.

This code applies in repository spaces and when someone is publicly
representing the project.
